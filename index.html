Who runs this anyway?

Magnolia. That’s the name I’m using for now. Privacy is a luxury, and honestly, the internet doesn't need to know everything about a fifteen-year-old with opinions.

I started The Fellowship because I was tired of committee-written corporate noise designed for clicks rather than clarity. I wanted a soul in the writing again.

It began as a personal project, but the name "Fellowship" is a promise. It's an invitation to anyone who's ever read a headline and thought, "Finally, someone said it." It’s for thinkers, skimmers, and everyone in between.
