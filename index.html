01 — The Editor
Who runs this anyway?

Magnolia.

That's what you can call me. Not my real name—privacy matters, and honestly, the internet doesn't need to know everything about a fifteen-year-old with opinions.

I started the Fellowship because I got tired of reading things that felt like they were written by committees and corporations. Things designed to make you click, not make you think. Things with no soul.

So I made my own thing.

The Fellowship is for thinkers. It's for skimmers. It's for anyone who's ever read something and thought "finally, someone said it."

That someone is me. And now, hopefully, us.
